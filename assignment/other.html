Guns, also known as firearms, are portable weapons that use explosive charges to propel projectiles. They are designed for various purposes, including hunting, sport shooting, self-defense, and military applications. Guns consist of several key components, including a barrel, action (which loads, fires, and ejects cartridges), stock (or grip), and trigger. They come in different types, such as rifles, shotguns, and handguns (pistols and revolvers), each with its own characteristics and uses. Guns require proper training and responsible handling to ensure safety and effectiveness.
